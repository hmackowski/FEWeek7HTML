PokéInfo

In the Pokémon games, Pokémon level up by gaining experience points (XP) through battles and defeating other Pokémon. As they accumulate enough XP, they increase their level, which boosts their stats and allows them to learn new moves. Additionally, some Pokémon have the ability to evolve, where they undergo a transformation into a different species with increased stats and sometimes even a change in their appearance. Evolution can occur through various means, such as reaching a certain level, using specific items, or forming a strong bond with their trainer.
